Pork Chop

Description

Pork chop is one of my best dishes. It is easy to cook, cheap, and very delicious. Give it a try!

Ingredients

  • Two slices of pork chop (1 portion)
  • Kimchi
  • Your choice of carbs
  • Your choice of vegetables

Steps

  1. Open two slices of pork chops.
  2. Heat frying pan on medium heat.
  3. Cook on one side for about 5 - 6 minutes.
  4. Flip over and cook for another 5 - 6 minutes.
  5. While pork chops are cooking, prepare plate with carbs, vegetables, and Kimchi.
  6. Check to make sure pork is at least 160F.
  7. Once cooked, remove pork chops from pan and sit on plate for 2 minutes.
  8. Serve!
