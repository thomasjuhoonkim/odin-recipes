Salmon Steak

Description

Salmon steak is one of my best dishes. It is easy to cook, cheap, and very delicious. Give it a try!

Ingredients

  • Two slices of salmon steak (1 portion)
  • Parsley
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Your choice of carbs
  • Your choice of vegetables

Steps

  1. Open a pack of salmon slices.
  2. Season with sufficient salt, pepper, and most importantly parsley!
  3. Heat frying pan to medium heat.
  4. Cook on skin side for about 8 - 10 minutes.
  5. Flip over and cook for about 2 - 3 minutes.
  6. Flip back to skin side and cook for 4 more minutes.
  7. While salmon is cooking, prepare your choice of carbs and vegetables.
  8. Remove from pan and let sit on plate for 3 minutes.
  9. Serve!
