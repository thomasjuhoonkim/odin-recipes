Korean Spicy Ramen

Description

Korean spicy ramen is one of my best dishes. It is easy to cook, cheap and very delicious. Give it a try!

Ingredients

  • A pack of Buldakke Ramen
  • Water
  • Alfredo Sauce

Steps

  1. Open Buldakke Ramen pack
  2. Boil 500 ml of water.
  3. Add noodles to boiling water and boil for 5 minutes.
  4. Once boiled, remove water and put loose noodles in a bowl.
  5. Add sauce and dried vegetables into the bowl and mix.
  6. Add 1 cup on alfredo sauce.
  7. Mix thoroughly and serve!
